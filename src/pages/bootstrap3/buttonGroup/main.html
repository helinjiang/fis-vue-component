2. 选项配置

2.1 bs-size

按钮组大小，String，非必须，可选值为：

  • （省略）：默认值，默认大小不用设置
  • lg：大按钮
  • sm：小按钮
  • xs：超小尺寸
left center right left center right left center right left center right 

    <bs3-button-group bs-size="lg">
        <bs3-button> left </bs3-button>
        <bs3-button> center </bs3-button>
        <bs3-button> right </bs3-button>
    </bs3-button-group>

    <bs3-button-group>
        <bs3-button> left </bs3-button>
        <bs3-button> center </bs3-button>
        <bs3-button> right </bs3-button>
    </bs3-button-group>

    <bs3-button-group bs-size="sm">
        <bs3-button> left </bs3-button>
        <bs3-button> center </bs3-button>
        <bs3-button> right </bs3-button>
    </bs3-button-group>

    <bs3-button-group bs-size="xs">
        <bs3-button> left </bs3-button>
        <bs3-button> center </bs3-button>
        <bs3-button> right </bs3-button>
    </bs3-button-group>

2.2 align

按钮组中按钮的排列方式，String，非必须，可选值为：

  • （省略）：默认值，横向排列
  • vertical：垂直排列
  • justified：两端对齐，但如果按钮是 <button>，则需要 特殊处理下，请看demo
left center right left center right left center right 

    <bs3-button-group>
        <bs3-button> left </bs3-button>
        <bs3-button> center </bs3-button>
        <bs3-button >right </bs3-button>
    </bs3-button-group>

    <bs3-button-group align="vertical">
        <bs3-button> left </bs3-button>
        <bs3-button> center </bs3-button>
        <bs3-button >right </bs3-button>
    </bs3-button-group>

    <bs3-button-group align="justified">
        <bs3-button-group><bs3-button> left </bs3-button></bs3-button-group>
        <bs3-button-group><bs3-button> center </bs3-button></bs3-button-group>
        <bs3-button-group><bs3-button >right </bs3-button></bs3-button-group>
    </bs3-button-group>
