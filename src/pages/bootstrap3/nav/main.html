略

2. 选项配置

2.1 bs-style

样式，String，非必须，可选值为：

  • tabs：默认值，标签页
  • pills：胶囊式标签页

Nav 导航中每一项菜单为 navItem 组件。navItem 组件可选参数为：

  • active：Boolean 类型，非必须，是否为激活的菜单项
  • disabled：Boolean 类型，非必须，是否为禁用的菜单项
  • dropdown：Boolean 类型，非必须，是否为下拉菜单
  • href：String 类型，非必须，跳转链接的href
  • target：String 类型，非必须，跳转链接的target
active link1 normal link disabled link link to qq.com dropdown action1 action2 action3
link1 link2 link3 

     <bs3-nav>
        <bs3-nav-item active>active link1</bs3-nav-item>
        <bs3-nav-item>normal link</bs3-nav-item>
        <bs3-nav-item disabled>disabled link</bs3-nav-item>
        <bs3-nav-item href="http://qq.com" target="_blank">link to qq.com</bs3-nav-item>
    </bs3-nav>

    <bs3-nav bs-style="pills">
        <bs3-nav-item active>link1</bs3-nav-item>
        <bs3-nav-item>link2</bs3-nav-item>
        <bs3-nav-item>link3</bs3-nav-item>
    </bs3-nav>

2.2 stacked

是否垂直方向堆叠排列，Boolean，非必须。注意，一旦设置了这个值，则也默认设置了 bs-style="pills"

link1 link2 link3 

    <bs3-nav stacked>
        <bs3-nav-item active>link1</bs3-nav-item>
        <bs3-nav-item>link2</bs3-nav-item>
        <bs3-nav-item>link3</bs3-nav-item>
    </bs3-nav>

2.3 justified

是否两端对齐，Boolean，非必须。

link1 link2 link3
link1 link2 link3 

    <bs3-nav justified>
        <bs3-nav-item active>link1</bs3-nav-item>
        <bs3-nav-item>link2</bs3-nav-item>
        <bs3-nav-item>link3</bs3-nav-item>
    </bs3-nav>
